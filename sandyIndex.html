About

Two right whales have already been killed by vessel strikes this year, bringing this vulnerable species closer to the brink of extinction. We cannot allow these agonizing deaths to continue. It’s time for the Biden administration to finalize the long-delayed vessel speed rule to protect critically endangered North Atlantic right whales from vessel strikes.
